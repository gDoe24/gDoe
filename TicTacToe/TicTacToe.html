Tic Tac Toe Game

  • Player 1 will be "X" and Player 2 will be "O"
  • Each player has to take turns until one player connects three in a row
  • The player able to draw three of their Xs or Os in a row wins
  • If all 9 positions are filled without a winner, the game is ends in a draw
  • You can start a new game at any point
